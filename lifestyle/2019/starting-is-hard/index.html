Manifesto

I made this blog for those curious about coding in Python, Machine Learning, Blockchain, those that don't have time for long tutorials or ridiculous bootcamps, 9-5 commuters with not a minute to spare, or anybody that wants to know more about technologies but feels stumped by a lack of time or structure, and keeps falling into bad habits.

My blog is designed to educate, inspire, and advise whenever and wherever we are in the world with Python, Machine Learning, and Blockchain through articles aimed at specific financial, data-driven, and interesting topics.
